Maternity & newborn · GTA · EN/Farsi

The challenge. Strong work, a genuine bilingual niche — but strangers couldn't tell in five seconds who it was for or how to book. Interest was created, then lost.

The strategy. One ideal client ("Sara"), a clear bilingual position, five content pillars weighted toward leads, a simple booking funnel, and a 30-day plan.

The outcome. A complete, ready-to-act Blueprint — bio rewrite, content calendar, and one clear booking path — turning a portfolio into a booking system.
